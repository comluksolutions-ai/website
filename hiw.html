How It Works

What This Product Is

The Comluk Digital Business Card is a simple, permanent online profile that replaces traditional paper business cards.

Each card opens a single, fast-loading page when someone scans your QR code.

From that one page, people can immediately see who you are, what you do, and contact you or visit your online presence with a single tap.

Your profile can include direct links to your phone, email, WhatsApp, website, and social media pages.

What Happens When Someone Scans the QR

Your digital business card opens instantly in their browser.

They can call, message, or visit your online pages with a single tap.

The page is fast, clear, and mobile-friendly.

How Profiles Are Built

Your profile is built using the information you submit through the intake form.

You provide your details once. There are no dashboards or ongoing setup required.

The layout is standardized to ensure long-term stability.

What Happens After You Purchase

Once your purchase is completed, Comluk Solutions manually confirms the transaction.

After confirmation, you receive a unique onboarding link to submit your details.

This allows us to review submissions for accuracy and deliver your profile correctly.

Hosting & Ownership

You choose where your digital business card lives.

No subscriptions. No lock-in.

Why This Simplicity Matters

The system is intentionally simple.

Once live, it keeps working year after year.

It does one job well: connecting people to you.
