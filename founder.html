Aeon O'Brian King

Company Founder

Designer of practical digital systems built for real-world use.

Aeon is a systems designer, engineer, and founder focused on building practical digital tools that work in the real world. He specializes in creating lightweight, durable systems that connect software, physical spaces, and human behavior—without unnecessary complexity.

His work spans QR-enabled digital identity platforms, vendor-connected kiosk systems, and interactive physical–digital games. Aeon is known for his methodical approach to system architecture, prioritizing clarity, reliability, and long-term maintainability over trend-driven solutions.

Through Comluk Solutions, he designs and delivers systems that help people organize information, coordinate action, and reduce friction between ideas and execution.
